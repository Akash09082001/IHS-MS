About Sheffield Hallam University UK
  • Where is the Sheffield Hallam University Located?
    Answer:

    Sheffield Hallam University (SHU) is a public research university in Sheffield, South Yorkshire, England. The university is based on two sites; the City Campus is located in the city centre near Sheffield railway station, while the Collegiate Crescent Campus is about two miles away in the Broomhall Estate off Ecclesall Road in south-west Sheffield.

  • What support services are available for international students at Sheffield Hallam University?
    Answer:

    SHU offers comprehensive support for international students, including airport pick-up services, orientation programs, academic support, career services, and access to the International Experience Team for visa and immigration advice.

  • When will I go to the Sheffield Hallam University?
    Answer:

    The Sheffield Hallam University will have 2 intakes for students coming through this route. The first intake of the year will be the January (Spring) intake and the final intake will be September (Fall) intake. Though the application process for the Sheffield Hallam University will start during the ITM Skills University online program, you will be able to go to the Sheffield Hallam University in the nearest intake which must be 3 months away from completion of your ITM Skills University PG Cert.

  • When will I get an Offer Letter from the Sheffield Hallam University?
    Answer:
    Sheffield Hallam University typically responds to online applications within a working day. After submitting your application and required documents, you can expect to receive a conditional offer letter relatively quickly. The exact timeline may vary, but the process is generally as follows:
    • You submit your application and documents to Sheffield Hallam University through their portal.
    • The university reviews your application.
    • If successful, you will receive a conditional offer letter via email.

    It's important to note that this initial offer is usually conditional, pending the fulfillment of certain requirements.

  • What is the list of documents which are required to make an application to the Sheffield Hallam University for a conditional offer letter?
    Answer:
    To apply for a conditional offer letter, you typically need to submit the following documents:
    • Completed application form
    • Academic transcripts or certificates from your previous studies
    • Proof of English language proficiency (IELTS scores)
    • Copy of your passport
    • One academic reference or letter of recommendation
    • Personal statement or statement of purpose
    • CV/Resume (if required for your specific program)
    • ITM Skills University (ISU) offer letter (as part of the ISU X LU MS Abroad Program)
  • What is the list of documents which are required to make an application to the Sheffield Hallam University for an unconditional offer letter?
    Answer:
    To convert your conditional offer to an unconditional offer, you will need to provide:
    • Final transcripts and certificates showing that you've met the academic requirements specified in your conditional offer
    • Proof that you've achieved the required English language proficiency score (IELTS 6.0 overall with a minimum of 5.5 in each component)
    • Evidence of sufficient funds to cover tuition fees and living expenses (as stipulated in the Visa norms)
    • Any additional documents requested in your conditional offer letter
    • Proof of completion of the PG Certificate from ITM Skills University with the required grades (typically 65% or above in each module)

    Once you've submitted these documents and they've been verified by Sheffield Hallam University, you should receive your unconditional offer letter. After receiving the unconditional offer, you can proceed with paying the required fees and applying for your Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS), which is necessary for your UK student visa application.
